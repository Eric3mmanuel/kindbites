At

we believe food is more than nourishment - it's Love ,joy and connection .Our mission is simple ,to spread kindness ,one plate at a time,one pi at a time. We believe that since pi is now part of our world , no one should ever again , sleep ,without having a decent meal .. the most basic of all human needs . Since pi network's project's launch , a lot of hope and good energy has been generated , and people from all over the globe , have restored their faith in the human race once again , through pi , which has given everyone , either rich or poor , regardless of race , gender , religion , physical status ..regardless of anything that has for centuries been dividing the people , a chance to begin a new , promoting fairness amongst the people of the earth. And thus ,at Kind Bites , we shall use Pi , as a tool to spread love and to make the world a better place .(We would like to take this chance to thank everyone involved in Pi for a good cause , we appreciate you and love you.Be blessed abundantly)

How we do this ,you ask ,,?

Well..
Here's how it works For every pioneer who buys a meal or a drink using pi and shares a meal with their loved ones - whether it's a friend , a spouse , a sibling , a parents or even a stranger - KindBites donates a matching meal and drink to someone in need
This could be a homeless person , an orphaned child or someone who has lost hope .Along with their meal , they also receive a beautiful free Kind Bites t-shirt ,with the Pi logo to help spread awareness in people about pi,as well as carrying a message of hope such as "Spread Love " - a small reminder that they are seen ,valued and cared for .
➡️If a pioneer(meaning anyone who pays with pi) shares one plate , one plate is donated .
➡️If a pioneer shares ten plates , five plates are donated ,by KindBites , to the needy .

Every bite that's shared creates a ripple of kindness that reaches beyond your table - into the hearts of those who need it most . Together ,we can make the world warmer ,kinder and more beautiful , through Pi . Always remember that

Kindness doesn't cost a dime 🫂, or a pi in our case 🤗💙
